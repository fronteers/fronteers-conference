Located in a picturesque historic street along one of Dordrecht’s canals, Stadshotel Steegoversloot offers a unique stay in a carefully renovated townhouse that blends heritage features with contemporary design. Each room has its own character, combining modern amenities with warm, inviting interiors that reflect the charm of the building’s history. Guests can step outside directly into Dordrecht’s old town, with its narrow streets, waterfront views, and a wide selection of cafés and restaurants within easy reach.

Thanks to its central location, the hotel is particularly convenient for visitors attending events in the city. Cinema de Witt is located approximately 230 metres away and can be reached in about 3 minutes on foot, making Stadshotel Steegoversloot an excellent base for a relaxed and walkable stay.
