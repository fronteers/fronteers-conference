Set in a former water tower on the edge of Dordrecht, Villa Augustus offers one of the most distinctive places to stay in the city. The hotel blends industrial architecture with a lush, almost Mediterranean-style garden environment, creating a calm and inspiring atmosphere. Rooms are spread across the tower and surrounding buildings, each with a minimalist yet warm design that emphasizes natural light, materials, and views of the greenery.

A highlight of the hotel is its expansive garden and greenhouse, where much of the produce for the on-site restaurant is grown. Guests can wander through the grounds, relax by the water, or enjoy a meal in a setting that feels more like a retreat than a typical city hotel.

While Villa Augustus is slightly outside the historic centre, the city is still easily accessible. Cinema de Witt can be reached in about 20–25 minutes on foot (approximately 1.5 kilometres), or much quicker by bike or taxi, making it a great option if you’re looking for a more peaceful and memorable stay during Fronteers Dark Mode.
