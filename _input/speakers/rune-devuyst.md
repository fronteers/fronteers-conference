[Rune Devuyst](https://www.linkedin.com/in/runedevuyst/) specialises in creating custom websites that are not only visually appealing and technically robust, but also deeply considerate of user needs. With a holistic approach to design and development, Rune partners closely with clients to identify key content and structure it strategically to meet business goals.

Technically, Rune brings deep expertise in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and VueJS—skills she uses to craft accessible, user-focused digital experiences. She is a Certified Professional in Accessibility Core Competencies (CPACC) and a strong advocate for inclusive design in every project.

Passionate about sharing knowledge, Rune regularly mentors others and leads workshops focused on accessibility, HTML, and CSS. She’s also known for her clear and practical educational videos that help developers and designers alike create better, more inclusive websites.

Whether she’s building a site from scratch, improving accessibility on an existing one, or teaching others how to do the same, Rune’s goal remains the same: to make the web a better place for everyone.
