Chris Lilley is a Technical Director at the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C). Considered “the father of SVG”, he also co-authored PNG, was co-editor of CSS2, chaired the group that developed @font-face, and co-developed WOFF and WOFF2. Ex Technical Architecture Group. Chris is co-editor of CSS Color levels 3, 4 and 5, and is the W3C representative to the International Color Consortium. Also, he's a recipient of [an Emmy Award](https://twitter.com/svgeesus/status/1518810391584804864) :-)
