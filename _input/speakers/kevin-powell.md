Writing CSS can quickly become a battle against the browser, as well as our own code. Maintenance can also be difficult as we layer things on. This talk explores how to work with the browser and use modern CSS solutions to create robust solutions with minimal CSS.

**[Kevin Powell](https://www.kevinpowell.co/)** is a CSS Evangelist who is passionate about helping front-end developers learn and appreciate CSS. He creates content on various platforms, including YouTube and Twitch, where he shares tips, tricks, tutorials, and live streams related to front-end web development and design. 

[Kevin's YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/kevinpowell) is the foundation of his work, offering in-depth explanations of fundamental concepts, exploring lesser-known CSS properties, and providing practical guidance on building web pages. 

[His Twitch streams](https://www.twitch.tv/kevinpowellcss) provide a more interactive experience, allowing viewers to witness problem-solving in real-time. 

Kevin's journey started with a background in film, followed by studies in Urban Studies and Graphic Design. He began creating web pages in the late 90s, combining Photoshop designs and HTML tables. While working as a designer, he ventured into freelance UI design, eventually discovering his passion for building websites. Teaching opportunities at a design school further solidified his expertise in web development. 

Seeking to expand his reach, Kevin started his YouTube channel to stay up to date, learn more, and share his knowledge. Today, his channel has become a successful platform where he offers courses and engages with a growing community of learners and experienced developers.
