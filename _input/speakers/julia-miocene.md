Julia Miocene is a Product Designer, UX Engineer, and Google Developer Expert on Web. She is best known as a Pure CSS Animator, pushing the limits of CSS to create things you wouldn’t believe pure CSS is capable of. Julia demonstrates the magic of CSS on her YouTube channel and through her demos. She also shares her knowledge of CSS and designing in the browser as a speaker.
