Coming to Fronteers Dark Mode from out of town? We’ve got you covered.

Dordrecht is a compact and charming city, which means most hotels are within walking distance (or a short bike ride) from Cinema de Witt, our venue. To make things easier, we’ve put together a small selection of hotels we can personally recommend, ranging from cozy boutique stays to more traditional options.

All listed accommodations offer a comfortable stay and good access to the city centre, so you’ll be close to restaurants, bars, and everything else you might want to explore before or after the event.

We recommend booking early, as Dordrecht isn’t huge and availability can be limited—especially around events.
